Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Qubi Toys Ltd · Bracknell, UK

👋
Who we are

Qubi Toys Ltd is a company based in Bracknell, UK. We are building Qubi — a privacy-first AI toy for children aged 3–10 that runs entirely on-device, with no cloud, no internet requirement, and no data leaving your home.

This privacy policy explains how we handle the personal data you provide when signing up to our waitlist at qubi-toys.co.uk.

📋
What data we collect

When you sign up to our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your first name and email address
  • Your child's approximate age group
  • Your country and approximate location (via IP address)
  • Your browser type and language preference
  • Your answers to our optional survey questions
💡 We collect location data only to understand where interest in Qubi is coming from geographically. We do not track or profile you beyond this.
🎯
Why we collect it

We use your data solely to:

  • Send you updates on Qubi's development progress
  • Notify you of our Kickstarter launch and early-bird pricing
  • Understand what parents care about so we build the right product
  • Estimate geographic demand to inform our launch strategy

The legal basis for processing your data is your consent, given when you submit the signup form.

🔒
How we store it

Your data is stored in Google Sheets, accessed only by the Qubi Toys Ltd team. Google's infrastructure is GDPR-compliant and your data is held within the European Economic Area.

We do not use any third-party marketing platforms, CRMs, or advertising tools. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties — ever.

🛡️ We practice what we preach. A company building a privacy-first product for children has no business treating your data carelessly.
📧
Communications

By signing up, you agree to receive occasional emails from us about Qubi's development. We will not spam you. You can expect:

  • Infrequent build updates (no more than once or twice a month)
  • A Kickstarter launch notification when we go live
  • Your early-bird discount code

You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email with "unsubscribe" or by emailing us directly.

✅
Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us and we will respond within 30 days.
